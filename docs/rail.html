5. Configure rail direction

Ok, your device is homing, or not if you so choose. But what if it is homing the wrong way? Oh crap, did you assemble it backwards? No, it can't be you...you're brilliant. It's gotta be those stupid instructions! Maybe you just think it is more aesthetically pleasing this way, or perhaps your device is double ended and you want an easy way to switch which end is the business end.

You've got two options:

1. Use the connect button below and toggle the switch.

2. While the device is blinking it's blue LED (after homing, but before entering any play modes), double click the function button on the control board. This is the one further from the LED.

In either case the OSSM will restart, and home itself in the new orientation...unless you have it set to not home, in which case... be careful!

Requirements: Chrome, Edge, or Opera on desktop (not mobile).
