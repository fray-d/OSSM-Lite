6. Name your device

You realize whenever you have this thing turned on it is broadcasting to the whole neighborhood that it exists, right? Maybe now's a good time to update the name of your device lest someone search the internet for OSSM and find themselves with a new kink.

Click the button below. Change the name to something with 8 characters or less. The device will reboot after you click save. It can take a little while for your computer or phone to learn the new name.

Requirements: Chrome, Edge, or Opera on desktop (not mobile).
