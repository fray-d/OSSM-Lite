OSSM Lite

10. Funscript Player

Select a video file, and a funscript. Set speed and depth limits. While the video is paused, min and max depth will move the OSSM to those positions. Device will not move if the speed limit is not set.

Requirements: Chrome, Edge, or Opera on desktop (not mobile).
